“As a native of the area who visits often, I've discovered a great lack of individual coffee shops with good coffee. The creme is not placed in the zeppoles until you order them. They are amazing and inexpensive! The bear claws and other pastries are very good too. Sometimes I may be too generous with a rating of 5, but in this case, 5 is not high enough. If I lived in the area, I'd be a regular.”
